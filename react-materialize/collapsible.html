Collapsibles are accordion elements that expand when clicked on. They allow you to hide content that is not immediately relevant to the user.

  • filter_dramaFirst
  • placeSecond
  • whatshotThird
<Collapsible popout>
  <CollapsibleItem header='First' icon='filter_drama'>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.
  </CollapsibleItem>
  <CollapsibleItem header='Second' icon='place'>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.
  </CollapsibleItem>
  <CollapsibleItem header='Third' icon='whatshot'>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.
  </CollapsibleItem>
</Collapsible>
NameTypeDefaultDescription
accordion
boolean
false

There are two ways a collapsible can behave. It can either allow multiple sections to stay open, or it can only allow one section to stay open at a time, which is called an accordion.
